Where can you find JUST B will it be?

This application will be able to develop children's emotional intelligence and provide statistics and data on how children live., It will give information for psychologists and exercises for parents that will help to understand emotionally difficult experiences and guide the child through a fulfilled and happy childhood.

In the FEELINGS section they will learn to differentiate between their emotions and feelings, and control their behaviour.

In the GAME section the child will navigate various stories, games and stories. The application will give us information on how they are living, if you feel uncomfortable with a family member, if you respond aggressively, how you are developing your emotional intelligence.
