What is Just B?

Just B is a tool that helps kids to develop and improve their emotional intelligence through a customized interactive cartoons.

Kids and parents will learn in an easy and fun way.

What is our objective?

Our goal is to develop an animated mirror so that children can be their own heroes and thus help them solve different problems. With this tool, children will learn to understand and manage their emotions, so they can grow to be emotionally present people as childhood is a crucial stage in learning how to manage and control their emotions.
